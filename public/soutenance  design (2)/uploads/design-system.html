05 · Principles
Design Principles
01 Canvas Ice for all backgrounds Prioritize #fbfdf6 as the primary page surface to maintain the light, airy feel that defines Adaline's visual tone.
02 Akkurat with -0.04em always Apply consistent negative letter spacing across all sizes. This is the signature precision that makes Adaline's typography immediately recognizable.
03 20px radius on interactive elements All buttons and navigation items use 20px border-radius. This creates a softer, more approachable interaction surface throughout the product.
04 Amber Seed for primary CTAs only Reserve the warm brown #4a3212 exclusively for primary action button backgrounds. Its uniqueness is what makes it work as a hierarchy signal.
05 Valley Green as the accent Use #203b14 for interactive states, icon fills, and subtle brand highlights. It is the single accent color — using it sparingly preserves its impact.
06 Flat surfaces, minimal elevation Avoid harsh shadows or strong elevation. Depth comes from layered flat surfaces and subtle border treatment, not drop shadows.
06 · Do's & Don'ts
Usage Guidelines

✓ Do

  • Prioritize Canvas Ice (#fbfdf6) for all primary backgrounds
  • Use Adaline Ink (#0a1d08) for headings and body text
  • Apply Akkurat with -0.04em letter spacing consistently
  • Use 20px border-radius on all interactive elements
  • Reserve Amber Seed (#4a3212) for primary CTA backgrounds only
  • Maintain 48px sectional gaps and 24px inner padding for cards
  • Use Valley Green (#203b14) for interactive states and icons

✗ Don't

  • Don't use highly saturated or vivid colors outside brand accent areas
  • Avoid harsh shadows or strong elevation — keep surfaces flat and layered
  • Don't introduce new typefaces beyond Akkurat and fragmentMono
  • Don't use square or minimal radius on buttons or nav items
  • Avoid dense packing — maintain comfortable element gaps (4px)
  • Don't use light text on light backgrounds for interactive elements
  • Don't use any solid button background color other than Amber Seed

08 · Imagery
Visual Language
Hero Backgrounds
Abstract digital-nature illustrations — rendered landscapes with misty mountains and serene lakes. Full-bleed, atmospheric, calm and contemplative.
Product Screenshots
UI screenshots as contained elements with slight rounding and minimal borders. Spacious, low-density presentation.
Icons
Minimal, monochromatic, outlined. Clean and functional. No filled or overly decorated icons — maintains the system's visual restraint.
